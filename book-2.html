The Primary Indicator of Transition to Unconstrained Power

The researchers identify the active subversion of judicial independence and the civil service as the most critical structural similarity — the clearest signal that an executive is moving from constrained to unconstrained rule.

GERMANY — 1933
THE ENABLING ACT

Passed March 23, 1933. Granted the cabinet power to bypass the Reichstag and the President to legislate. The deliberative legislature was effectively abolished in a single vote.

Judiciary: "Synchronized" — all critical courts neutralised

Source: Enabling Act of 1933 — Wikipedia

USA — 2025–2026

Reclassifies tens of thousands of career civil servants as "at-will" employees — allowing arbitrary removal and replacement with loyalists. Officially renamed "Schedule Policy/Career."
